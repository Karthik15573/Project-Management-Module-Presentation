# Analyzing Human and Financial Resource Management: UK Banking Sector

**Project Type:** Group Module Assessment Presentation

## Overview
This group project explores human and financial resource management practices in the UK banking sector. The study focuses on workforce planning, budgeting, resource allocation, and performance management to understand how banks optimize resources for efficiency and growth.

## Objectives
- Analyze human resource strategies and financial resource planning in UK banks  
- Examine the impact of resource management on organizational performance  
- Apply project management principles in preparing and delivering a structured group presentation  

## Key Sections in Presentation
- Overview of the UK banking sector  
- Human resource management practices  
- Financial resource management practices  
- Analysis of challenges and best practices  
- Recommendations for improved efficiency
